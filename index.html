POPSHOP is a working space and learning environment open to the public. Designed for programmed exchange and chance encounters, POPSHOP is a launchpad for start-ups and ideas.

It’s about narrowing the gaps between people, projects, and disciplines, it’s about embracing change and making connections where none seem to exist.

It’s about engaging the local start-up community to do epic and make the world a better place.

From VC’s and angel investors to prototypes and science-fair projects, come see what everyone is working on and learn what you need to know to build a successful start-up.
